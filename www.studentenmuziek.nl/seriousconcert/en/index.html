Charity concert!

Welcome to the website of Serious Concert.

Serious Concert is a concert to raise funding for charity and took place on Tuesday the 21st of December 8.15 p.m. in Muziekgebouw Frits Philips in Eindhoven. It is an initiative of Eindhovens Studenten Muziek Gezelschap Quadrivium, the classical music association of TU/e students.
