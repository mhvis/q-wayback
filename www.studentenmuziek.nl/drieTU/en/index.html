One weekend, three orchestras

During one weekend, the Student Wind Orchestra Twente (SHOT), the symphony orchestra of the Delft Student Music Society Krashna Musika and wind orchestra Auletes of the Eindhoven Student Music Society Quadrivium gave three joint concerts in their three home cities. During each concert, the three orchestras performed in sequence, each giving an own special contribution to the programme. The society that is played in its own university town played an encore as well.


drieTUconcerten wins Student Union Cultuurprijs

Recently the project 'drieTUconcerten' is awarded with the Student Union Cultuurprijs, a price for stimulating the cultural climat at the campus.
