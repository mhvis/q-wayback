Programme

The different compositions of the three orchestras provide the possibility to present a diverse programme. The repertoire included both symphonic music, played by the symphony and wind orchestras, as well as original music for wind orchestras.
Each evening, the same basic programme was performed. The orchestra that played last on the concert evening, played an additional piece.
