Goal Organization

Goal

  • To offer a professional outdoor platform for Eindhoven region classical music, for both professionals and amateurs
  • To strengthen the connections between amateur and professional musicians by promoting knowledge transfoer, through (among others) creating opportunities for collaborations, workshops and masterclasses for talent development
  • To increase the interaction with the public through offering surprising and accessible concerts, which bring together multiple institutions and organizations from the Eindhoven region.
  • To make classical music accessible in general to a broader audience, through interaciton with crossover projects, education, collaboration, and participation projects.
  • To create a partly-professional high quality programme intended to create connections with different disciplines and areas of innovative value
