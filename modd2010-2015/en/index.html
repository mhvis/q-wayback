Welcome to Muziek op de Dommel

The 5th edition of Eindhoven's largest classical music festival will take place on May 31 and June 1, 2014.

A new location, and a different program:
for the first time ever, we're moving camp to the TU/e campus, where we'll be surrounded by the beautiful green Dommel river area.

Saturday will feature workshops in the morning (taught by the professional musicians of the philharmonie zuidnederland) and concerts through the afternoon. To close the day off, we have a special concert: The Website Story. Sunday morning will see the opening of the musical event "Doe de Dommel". The afternoon is filled with Kamermuziek aan de Dommel. This will include a special Kindermuziektuin (children's music garden) full of musical games, activities, and puzzles.

Muziek op de Dommel is for everyone from young to old, and is free to attend. Come join us for some musical enjoyment in a lovely green outdoor environment.
