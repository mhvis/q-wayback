Enjoy a special evening during the Dommelconcert "The Website Story"

New and renewing: a special love story comes to life during the Dommel concert "The Website Story". An impossible love that begins and ends on the internet. The internet, source of information and unbounded data traffice, but also the source of problems wiht privacy and identify fraud. These issues and more will play a role in the concert through animations, music, and a light spectacle. To bring more power to the story, the public is invited to download our The Website Story app. Through the app, the public will be playing a part in the concert!
