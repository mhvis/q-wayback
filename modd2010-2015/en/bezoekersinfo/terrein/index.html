Entrance

Entrance to the festival grounds and performance is free.

Festival Muziek op de Dommel is taking place on the TU Eindhoven campus this year for the first time. The campus lies in the center of Eindhoven, a ten minute walk from Eindhoven train station. A route description and map of the campus is available at: http://www.tue.nl/universiteit/over-de-universiteit/route-en-plattegrond/

Parking on the TU/e campus is free on Saturday May 31 and Sunday June 1
