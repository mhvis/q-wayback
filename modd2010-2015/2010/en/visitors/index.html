Visitors

The festival is located in the city center of Eindhoven. At the backside of the Van Abbemuseum the stage is floating at the Dommel. At this stage the choirs and orchestras will give their concert.

The festival has a no entrance fee.

By the central location in the city center of Eindhoven the festival is easily accessible by car, bus and rail.

By car

Eindhoven is situated at the junction of the A2 and A67 motorways. No matter how you approach the city, head to the city centre and follow the signs to the Car Park 'Stadskantoor/Van Abbemuseum', Car Park Stadhuisplein, Heuvelgalerie, or De Lage Landen car park, which are all a five minute walk away from the festival. These are the most convenient places to park your car.

By train

Every half hour, Dutch Railways operates city-to-city links from Amsterdam, Utrecht, The Hague, Rotterdam, Venlo, Heerlen and Maastricht to Eindhoven. The festival is a ten minute walk from the train station through the city centre.

By bus

The bus station is located next to the Eindhoven train station. Bus line 7 and 17 will take you directly to the festival within ten minutes. Get off the bus at the 'Stadhuisplein' stop. Bus line 10, 12, 20, 24, 170, 171, 172 and 173 also take you to the festival. Get off the bus at 'Smalle Haven' stop

On foot

From the train station it's a ten minute walk to the Van Abbemuseum through the inner city.
