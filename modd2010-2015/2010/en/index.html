5th of June: 'Music at the Dommel'

Stage at the Dommel
Van Abbemuseum, Eindhoven
Free entrance

Photo's of the festival

View the first pictures of the festival!

Saturday the fifth of June music sounded everywhere in the city center of Eindhoven. Choirs and orchestras from the whole area performed at an enormous stage, at a unique location - on the water! - at the Dommel near the Van Abbemuseum. Lots of people came to the festival and enjoyed the music.

Music at the Dommel, an amazing festival you cannot miss!
