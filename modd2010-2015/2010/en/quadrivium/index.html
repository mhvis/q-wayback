Quadrivium

ESMG Quadrivium is the music association for students in Eindhoven. For all of the 140 members classical music plays an important role. In the wind orchestra, the symphonic orchestra, the recorder ensemble, the choir or as a piano member they all make music. During their concerts they mostly perform(modern) classical music, sometimes accompanying (professional) soloists

Quadrivium is an association which tries to explore its limits, in music and in organization. The enthusiastic members (all students from University of Technology Eindhoven and Fontys Hogescholen) have organized several great projects in the past, like the performance of the Carmina Burana and the Queen Symphony in Muziekcentrum Frits Philips Eindhoven, several music contests for amateur soloists and a concert tour through Tuscany, Italy.
