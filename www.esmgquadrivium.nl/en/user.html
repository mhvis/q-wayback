The member area is currently only available in Dutch. On this page we will explain how you can log in on the Dutch site and what you can find there, so that also non-Dutch speaking members can use the information that can be found there.

To log in, go to nederlands in the menu and then click on inloggen. Under Gebruikersnaam: and Wachtwoord: fill in the username and password, respectively, that can be found in each newsletter. Then click on Inloggen.

The following information can be found in the member area:

  • Muziek: Music files of the music that is currently being rehearsed.
  • Repetities: Schedules of the upcoming rehearsels. (Not always up-to-date)
  • ALV: The documents that are going to be treated on the next official members meeting. (often only in Dutch)
  • Leden: Lists with information about all members. Huidig contains information such as (email) addresses and phone numbers. Verjaardag contains a birthday calandar.
  • Mailadressen: An overview of all important email adresses.
  • Vereniging: Official documents of Quadrivium.
  • Lustrumboek: The books of the past lustrums in pdf format.
  • Pressto: pdf files of all Pressto's (Quadrivium's magazine) since 2001.
  • Vrienden mg: As a member of Quadrivium you can visit certain concerts for a reduced price. This page gives an overview of those concerts.
  • Externe promo: Information about activities that are not organized by Quadrivium, but that might be interesting for its members.
  • Uitloggen: Log out.
