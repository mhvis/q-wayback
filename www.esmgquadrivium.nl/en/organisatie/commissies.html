Below is a survey of the different committees within Quadrivium. If you are interested in a committee or if you want to know more, email them.  Emailaddresses can be found after logging in in the members part.

The AuleCie is the committee which supports the commissioner Auletes.

This committee consists of:

  • Laurens van Heesch
  • Sabine Jongerius
  • Lars van Ruremonde
  • Marjolein de Munnik
  • Annie Moeskops

AQaQuCie

The AQaQuCie provides something tasty during the ALV. This committee consists of the following cookie bakers:

  • Renée van Kemenade

This committee consists of:
